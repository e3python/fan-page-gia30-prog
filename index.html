Plot Summary

Miss Peregrins Home for Peculiar Childeren is a 6 book series that follows the main character Jacob as he learns how to live life after finding out he can see things others cant. He doesnt have the luxury to make mistakes with his ability because it would mean the end for him and all his companions, and maybe even the whole peculiar world as he knows it.

Books is the series

  1. Miss Peregrins Home for Peculiar Children
  2. Hollow City
  3. Library of Souls
  4. A Map of Days
  5. The Conference of the Birds
  6. The Desolations of Devil's Acre
